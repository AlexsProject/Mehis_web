## Competitor Pricing

| Service | Price | Notes |
|---------|-------|-------|
| **Shimmer Care** | $140-345/mo | Human coaching, expensive |
| **Inflow (Forbes)** | $22.49/mo | App-based, clinician-designed |
| **ADHD Online** | Varies | Online coaching |
| **Average Coach** | $75-200/session | $300-600/mo for coaching |

## Target Audience
- Parents of children with ADHD (ages 5-15)
- Often overwhelmed, looking for quick help
- Value practicality over theory
- Active in Facebook groups, Reddit (r/adhd)
